<p>Dear participants</p>
    <p>You are invited to participate in a research study exploring how individuals attribute moral responsibility. If you agree to participate, you will read a short story about an individual named Tom, Mark, or John. You will then be asked to provide ratings regarding their actions and character. The survey will take approximately 6 minutes to complete.</p>
    <br>
<br>
    <h3>Purpose of the Study</h3>
    <p>The purpose of this study is scientific. The researchers involved in this study are academic researchers associated with the University of Zurich and Oldenburg. The responsible researcher for this project is Dr. Pascale Willemsen.</p>
    <br>
<br>
    <h3>Nature of the Stimuli (Trigger Warning)</h3>
    <p>This research study examines moral judgments regarding social interactions. Please be advised that the survey <b> may contain stories depicting prejudice and mistreatment of marginalized groups.</b> These scenarios are designed to be realistic and <b> may be distressing or offensive to some participants.</b> If you believe that exposure to content involving racism, homophobia, or sexual harassment may cause you any distress, please do not begin the survey. Your participation is entirely voluntary, and you may withdraw at any time without penalty by closing your browser.</p>
    <br>
<br>
    
    <h3>Voluntary Participation and Withdrawal</h3>
    <p>Your participation is entirely voluntary. You have the right to withdraw from the study at any time, for any reason, without any penalty or loss of benefits.
